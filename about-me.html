About me

Hey, my name is Kim Wauben (22) July 2018 I graduated as a fashion designer from a school in the Netherlands.
Fashion has always been a passion of mine. This started with making clothing and after that I started creating technical drawings.
Besides my passion for fashion design I developed a passion for photography. For now as a hobby but I would love to expand my knowledge. I have created a lot of technical knowledge over the last couple of years.
I love to work with programs like Illustrator, Photoshop, Indesign and silhouette studio I'm all round and always open to learning something new
